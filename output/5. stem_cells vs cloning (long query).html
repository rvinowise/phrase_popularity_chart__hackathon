in 2007 'Stem Cells' surpassed 'Cloning' in both: Scientific papers and literature

Pubmed: https://esperr.github.io/pubmed-by-year
Europe PMC: https://europepmc.org
Books: https://books.google.com/ngrams

Formula

Pubmed relative = articles on Pumbed with the searched phrase / total articles on Pumbed in that year
PMC relative to Pubmed's total = articles on EuropePMC with the searched phrase / total articles on Pumbed in that year (because EuropePMC doesn't provide its total articles per year)
Books = amount of the searched phrase relative to all other words in all books
in order to equalise the amounts of articles with the amount of words, the amount of articles is divided by 4100 (average words in an article) and multiplied by 5 (average word repetition in an article)
